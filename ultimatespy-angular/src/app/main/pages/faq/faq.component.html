2. About Operations

How can I find the latest hot ads?
You can go to “Featured Ads” which are picked by big data and intelligence, and you can discover new ideas tracked by thousands of users from “People Tracked”as well.
How would featured ads be sorted?
It can be sorted by filters like “Newest”, “Like”, “Comments” and “Shares”.
How many ads can I track?
Basic user can track 10 creatives daily; Premium user can track unlimited creatives daily. Even if you delete some ads tracked, tracking time will still be counted.

3. About Payment

Can I pay with PayPal?
Yes, currently we only support Paypal.
How to understand the number of tracking?
Each time you track ad, the number of track is counted once. When you run out daily tracking quota, you will not be able to track until the tracking quota is reset in next day. Or you can get more tracking times by upgrading.

Can I cancel anytime?
Yes, you can cancel your subscription whenever you'd like and continue to use AdsCrawlr for the remainder of your billing cycle. We will not charge after the expiry date.
How can we cancel auto-renewing subscriptions?
You can click your account in the top right conner and go to “My plan—> Plan”  to cancel it.
How to upgrade ?
If you want to upgrade your plan, you can go to the “Pricing” page, choose more advanced plan and pay the price differences. Or you can go to “My plan—> Plan” to upgrade it.

Each time you use searching, filtering, sorting or paging, the number of queries is counted once.When you run out daily quota, you will not be able to query until the quota is reset in next day. Or you can get more queries by upgrading.
