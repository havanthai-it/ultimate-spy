2. About Operations

How can I find the latest hot ads?
You can go to “Featured Ads” which are picked by big data and intelligence, and you can discover new ideas tracked by thousands of users from “People Tracked”as well.
Why do I get results with some products I’m not interested in?
With the same keyword, it would be added in different ads creatives, you can exclude the product by filling the name in the “Exclude in result” field, and click “Submit”
Why can I only view ads on Facebook?
As for Free members, only ads on Facebook are accessible; for the Basic plan, Facebook and Instagram ads are available; for the Pro plan, all six networks can access. If you want to view more networks,please upgrade.
How would featured ads be sorted?
It can be sorted by filters like ”Last Seen”, “First Seen”, “Like”, “Comments” and “Shares”.
Can I check featured ads in other networks?
We just offer featured ads on facebook. People tracked ads are based on all six networks.
How can I access to the original ads?
You can click the title of ads interested you and it will turn to the original ads on the network.
How many creatives can I download？
Basic users can download 25 creatives per day; Pro users can download 250 creatives per day.in the elite plan, 999 downloads daily.
How many ads can I track?
Basic user can track 25 creatives daily; Pro user can track 250 creatives daily. in the elite plan, 999 downloads daily. Even if you delete some ads tracked, tracking time will still be counted.
For the tracked ads, what information can I obtain？
After tracking, you can access to the trend of ads easily, like the period and performance variation.
Can I check the rank of advertisers?
Sure, you can upgrade to ELITE plan, then just go to "Top Charts" to check it.

3. About Payment

Can I pay with PayPal?
Sorry, only credit card and Alipay is acceptable.
How to understand the number of downloads?
Each time you download creatives, the number of downloads is counted once.When you run out daily quota, you will not be able to download until the quota is reset in next day. Or you can get more downloads by upgrading.
How to understand the number of tracking?
Each time you track ad, the number of track is counted once. When you run out daily tracking quota, you will not be able to track until the tracking quota is reset in next day. Or you can get more tracking times by upgrading.
